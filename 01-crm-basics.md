# CRM Basics in Salesforce

## What is CRM?

CRM means Customer Relationship Management.

Salesforce CRM helps businesses manage leads, customers, sales, follow-ups, opportunities, and business relationships in one system.

## Main Salesforce Objects

### 1. Lead

A Lead is a potential customer who has shown interest but is not yet confirmed.

Example:
Someone fills a consultation form or asks about a product.

### 2. Account

An Account represents a company or customer profile.

Example:
A customer record or business entity.

### 3. Contact

A Contact is a person related to an Account.

Example:
Customer name, phone number, email, etc.

### 4. Opportunity

An Opportunity represents a possible sale.

Example:
A customer is interested in buying a wellness plan or product.

## Basic Sales Flow

Lead Created  
↓  
Lead Qualified  
↓  
Converted into Account + Contact + Opportunity  
↓  
Follow-up  
↓  
Order / Sale Closed
